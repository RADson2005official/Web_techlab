Our Story

Our History

A legacy of precision since 1985

Timeless was founded by master clockmaker Theodore Wells with a simple mission: to create timepieces that combine traditional craftsmanship with innovative design. What began as a small workshop has grown into a respected name in horology, while maintaining our commitment to quality and precision.

For over three decades, we've been crafting clocks that aren't just functional objects but pieces of art that transform spaces and become family heirlooms.
